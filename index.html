I am a Lecturer in the Faculty of Information Technology and Computer Science at University of Central Punjab, Lahore. I mainly teach courses related to Programming, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. My research work involves developing machine learning algorithms with better generalization, robustness and explainability. I apply ML to improve healthcare, industry and climate.

I received my B.S in Electrical Engineering from University of Engineering and Technology, Lahore and M.S in Computer Engineering from National University of Sciences and Technology, Islamabad.
